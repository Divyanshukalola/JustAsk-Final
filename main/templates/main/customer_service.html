Customer Service

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I cancel my subscription plan.

    You can cancel your subscription plan by going to your profile and clicking your username in the navigation panel or you can email us on support@just-ask.in for canceling the plan and we will revert back within 24 hours from delivery (Your email should include your Student ID that Just Ask provided you with your full name). Once you decide to cancel the subscription plan please consider this that you cannot go back you have to subscribe again in order to use our services.

  • Where are my E-books?

    Once you have purchased an E-book the book will be visible under your collection. Click books from the navigation panel and you will see your collection or you can go to your profile by clicking your username from the navigation panel to view the same.

  • How to access book solutions?

    If a book is equiped with a solution than it will be visible under the book that you are viewing. This option depends whether the book has a solutions attached to it.

  • How do I contact Just Ask?

    You can fill out the form below or you can email us on support@just-ask.in. Please read our terms and conditions before emailing us. Please refrain yourself from using sensitive language.

  • What do I gain from contributing my notes?

    Just Ask do not pay the contributors for uploading their notes to Just Ask but the notes that are uploaded will be displayed under the contributor's name. All though special credits are given to the contributor if he/she decides to upload their resources.

  • Will I be charged for using grammer check from Just Ask?

    Checking your grammer is a free service from Just Ask. You do not have to pay for using this service.

  • How do I earn credits?

    Credits are earned by either subscribing to JustAsk, contributing notes or they can also be earned by posting and answering a question. You will only earn credits on posting a question if that question is answered. The person who answers the question also earns credits as well as the person who posts it.

  • What will happen if I have 2000 credits?

    Once you reach the limit of credits which is 2000 you cannot earn more than the limit. You have to redeem your credits in order to continue earning from the questions that you post.

  • Can I redeem credits if I have not subscribed?

    You cannot redeem credits if your subscription is ended. You have to subscribe in order to use your credits.

  • How to change the Music Playlist?

    Go to your profile and click on "PlayList" and copy past the Spotify PlayList that you want to add.
